Creator: Alice Alves

About: Developed as coursework for module Graphics 1, during the 2nd year of course Computer Graphics, Imaging and Multimedia at University of East Anglia. Developed in C++ using OpenGL.

Controls:
* right/left arrow key - move right/left
* up arrow keys - jump
* space - pause/unpause

Objective of the game is to collect all 7 colours and reach the portal. Coming in contact with enemies or jumping off screen will make player lose a life. If there are no more lives left it will be game over. Enemies can be defeated by jumping on top of them. Certain elements of the game won't work until a specific colour has been collected.
